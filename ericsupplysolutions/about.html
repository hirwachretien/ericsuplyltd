About ERI SUPPLY

Supplying Excellence, Delivering Trust

Company Overview

Founded on June 20th, 2023, ERI SUPPLY SOLUTIONS LTD is a forward-thinking Rwandan company specializing in delivering high-quality products and services in power and electricity, IT infrastructure, plumbing, HVAC systems, engineering, furniture, and construction materials. Driven by innovation, reliability, and professionalism, we are committed to empowering businesses and institutions through efficient supply chains and sustainable solutions. Our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for both public and private sector clients in Rwanda and beyond.

Our Mission

To deliver dependable, cost-effective, and timely supply and service solutions that support the smooth functioning of businesses and institutions.

Our Vision

To become a leading multi-sector service provider in Rwanda and beyond, recognized for quality products, reliable service, and professional integrity.
